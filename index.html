My Story!

Nur Yilmaz Ruppi is a Turkish-Italian architect, interior designer, and journalist with a diverse cultural background, embracing a cosmopolitan identity. Her holistic work philosophy, employs a complete 360Â° approach. She meticulously analyses her clients' lifestyles to invent environments with ambience for both their workspaces and living areas. Born in Istanbul, a melting pot of diverse cultures, Nur spent her formative years in the picturesque city of Tekirdag, near Istanbul, along the shores of the Marmara Sea. Her passion and desire in decor and interior design blossomed her interest during her childhood years. Nur pursued her architectural studies in Istanbul, to then refine her skills in various reputable furniture companies. After completing her studies, she spent a year each in London and Florence, gaining valuable experience and aesthetic knowledge in her field. In Turkey, she met her husband, and together, they decided to relocate to Barcelona, Spain, in order to broaden, strengthen, and expand their horizons. There, Nur contributed her expertise to various Spanish hotel projects while working for a Turkish furniture company. Several years later, she moved to Brussels, where she started a family. This is where she not only raised her two sons but also continued writing articles for different interior magazines. Nur's deeply rooted passion is the key and driving force of her love for her work. This stems from her culturally diverse background, and various cultures and art she has surrounded herself with. Her inspiration and passion for interior design and her costumed designs are sparked by the diverse and artisan fabrics and art and design objects, she finds things that attract her during her daily pursuits and travels. Nur's journey then led her to Italy, where she and her family chose to settle in the tranquil town of Vicenza. Currently, she continues her explorations, seeking new ideas and inspiration by the calming sound of the ocean when she is at the seaside, nearby Venice. One of her dreams is to travel with her family in a camper and reminiscent and share her childhood adventures. Nur Yilmaz Ruppi is not just an architect and designer; she is a global citizen whose diverse experiences contribute to the depth and uniqueness of her creative endeavours. Her generosity and warm heart make the ideal person to create a beautiful atmosphere, with style and class.
